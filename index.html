Our Story

The name Study Circle comes from a story of learning and success. It was first the name of a coaching center run by my respected English teacher, Jahurul Haque Sir, when I was a student at Kurigram River View High School. I studied there, and with his guidance, I achieved a very good result in my SSC exam, securing First Division with 5 Star marks, scoring 839 marks across 10 subjects.

Inspired by that experience, I started Study Circle again with a new goal. I want to help students build strong skills through simple explanations, regular practice, and proper guidance. I also use modern technology and better teaching methods to make learning easy, effective, and interesting for today’s students.

I would also like to express my heartfelt gratitude to all my teachers. Their support, care, and inspiration have helped me become who I am today. I try to follow their values and use their lessons to guide my own students towards success.

About Us

I’m Tajul Islam Bipul,

a passionate and dedicated educator with strong experience in the teaching profession. I hold a B.Sc. (Honours) and M.Sc. in Mathematics. I focus on concept-driven learning, clear explanations, and continuous assessment to help students build a strong academic foundation. My teaching approach is interactive and student-centered, aiming to boost confidence, critical thinking, and deep understanding rather than rote memorization.

Alongside teaching, I am a full-stack web developer with hands-on experience in modern web technologies. I specialize in website design and development using JavaScript, React, Node.js, and MongoDB. I enjoy building responsive, user-friendly, and high-performance web applications. This combination of teaching and technical expertise allows me to bring a modern, practical perspective to education—making learning more engaging, relevant, and aligned with today’s digital world.

Vision & Objectives

At Study Circle Academic Coaching Center, our mission is to provide a comprehensive and engaging learning experience that helps students excel in academics while building critical thinking, confidence, and study skills.

Our Vision

We envision a future where every student of Study Circle Academic Coaching Center is confident, knowledgeable, and fully prepared for their exams. Our goal is to make learning effective, enjoyable, and result-oriented.

Our Objectives

  • Expert Guidance: Experienced teachers provide clear explanations and exam-oriented strategies.
  • Comprehensive Syllabus Coverage: Every topic is covered thoroughly with examples and practice questions.
  • Practice Worksheets: Students get ample worksheets for self-study and revision.
  • Exam Preparation: Regular mock tests and solved past papers for better exam readiness.
  • Reading & Writing Skills: Focused exercises to improve language and comprehension.
  • Interactive Learning: Classroom discussions and problem-solving sessions to engage students actively.
  • Progress Tracking: Continuous assessment and feedback for each student.
  • Time Management Skills: Tips and strategies to manage study time efficiently.
  • Supportive Environment: Encouragement and guidance to build confidence in students.
  • Motivation & Career Guidance: Advice to help students plan their academic journey and future goals.

At Study Circle Academic Coaching, we are committed to helping students achieve their full potential and build a strong foundation for success in life and exams.
